Dementia Early Detection App

Detect memory problems early. Support families.

Problem

Dementia is detected very late in India. Early memory loss is ignored as normal ageing. This leads to irreversible damage and heavy family burden.

Our Solution

A mobile app that tracks memory, speech, behaviour, and caregiver observations to detect early warning signs of dementia.
